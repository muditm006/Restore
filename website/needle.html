Back to Healing Page

Bridge

If you can, begin in a standing position and backbend down into a. If not, lay down on your back and push with your hands and feet into a bridge. Once in a bridge, straighten your legs and rock slowly back and forth for 15 secs. Then, walk your feet and arms as close together as you can. Bend and straighten your legs 5 times, keeping the curvature of your spine.

Down and Up Backbends

Slowly bend back into a backbend and then slowly straighten up. Bend your back as much as possible each time.

Scorpion Split

With your hips squared straight and legs turned out, place both your hands straight down on the ground. Keeping your eyes looking straight ahead, slowly bend backwards as far as you can. Bend your back leg and hold it with one or both of your hands. Hold for 15 secs. Feel free to use a wall to help you.
