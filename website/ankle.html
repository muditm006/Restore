Ankle Exercises

Back to Healing Page

Heel Slides

Slide the heel backwards, keeping the foot flat on the floor. Hold for a few seconds and repeat for each leg for a duration of 30 seconds each.

Foot Pulls

Grasp the middle of your foot and slowly pull the foot downwards to stretch the front of the ankle. Hold for 30 seconds per foot.

Passive Ankle Stretches

Grasp the outer border of your foot and gently draw the foot upwards and inwards as if trying to turn up the sole of your foot until you feel a stretch down the outer side of your ankle. Hold for 30 seconds per foot.

Heel Sit Backs

Slowly, sit back onto your heels until the ankle stretches at the front. Hold for a minute per foot.
