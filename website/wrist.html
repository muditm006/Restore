Wrist Exercises

Back to Healing Page

Finger Stretches

With your hand out in front, gently pull back each finger one by one. Swap and stretch the fingers on the other hand. To stretch the palm of your hand, use one hand to pull back on all the fingers of your other hand at once. Swap hands. Make a fist with both hands and open slowly to stretch the fingers and thumbs as far as possible. Complete this process for a duration of a minute per hand.

Finger Bends

Hold your hands out front with a vertical palm and thumbs pointing up. Next, bend your right thumb toward your palm and hold for a couple of seconds before straightening your thumb back up. Bend your index finger toward your palm (while trying to keep your other fingers straight) and hold for a couple of seconds. Repeat with each finger and thumb on the other hand. Complete this process for a duration of a minute per hand.

Loosen-up Stretch

Sit comfortably and bend your arm at the elbow at a right angle. Make a fist, and then slowly open it, spreading and stretching your fingers apart. Complete this stretch for a duration of 30 seconds. Repeat with your other hand.
